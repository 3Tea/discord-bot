## Welcome

3AT is a multi-system Discord companion for economy, XP, quests, and mini-games. This guide is a quick tour of everything available — each section links to a dedicated guide with full details. Dive in anywhere, or follow along from top to bottom.

## Earning Your First Coins

The fastest way to start earning is the `/pray` command — a daily action that rewards **50–200 coins** depending on how you use it.

- `/pray` — self pray for 50–150 coins every 24 hours
- `/pray target:@user` — pray for another user for 100–200 coins **plus a 5% gem chance**
- `/curse` — a second daily action with its own separate cooldown (20–100 coins)

**Streak tip:** Pray on consecutive days to build a streak. Hitting 3, 7, 14, and 30 consecutive days unlocks bonus coin and gem rewards.

> **Tip:** Always pray for someone else when possible — higher rewards and a chance to earn gems!

For full details, see the [Economy Guide](/en/guide/economy).

## Working & Fishing

Two more reliable income sources that run on cooldowns:

- `/work` — earn **80–200 coins** every 4 hours. Pick a random job, collect your pay. Simple steady income with no luck involved.
- `/fish` — cast your line every **hour** for fish with 4 rarity tiers. Common catches (55%) earn 10–30 coins; legendary catches (4%) can reward 300–600 coins.

See the [Economy Guide](/en/guide/economy) for cooldown configuration details.

## Checking Your Progress

Keep tabs on your earnings and server standing:

- `/balance` — see your current coins, gems, pray streak, and last activity
- `/rank` — view your XP level, progress bar, and where you rank in the server
- `/profile` — view a full profile card combining level, economy, streaks, and activity in one place (canvas image for premium users)
- `/achievements` — browse 50 achievements across 10 categories; milestones unlock automatic coin, gem, and star rewards

Both commands accept an optional `user:@someone` to check another member's stats.

## The Shop

Each server has its own shop configured by admins. Browse and buy with:

- `/shop view` — see all available items, prices, and stock
- `/shop buy` — purchase an item using coins or gems

Item types include **roles** (a Discord role assigned to you), **cosmetics**, and **currency exchanges**. Stock is limited — popular items sell out fast.

See the [Economy Guide](/en/guide/economy) for the full shop breakdown.

## Gambling

Put your coins to work in the casino:

- `/gamble coinflip` — 50/50 double or nothing. The fairest game with no house edge.
- `/gamble slots` — match symbols for up to ×20 payout. Higher risk, higher reward.
- `/gamble dice` — guess high or low on a 2d6 roll.

> **Warning:** Slots and dice have a house edge built in. Gambling is designed as a coin sink — the server economy is more fun when coins don't pile up infinitely. Bet responsibly.

See the [Economy Guide](/en/guide/economy) for bet limits and cooldowns.

## Mining & Dungeon

Two mini-games for adventurous earners:

### /mine
Dig underground for minerals every **2 hours**. Five mineral tiers — from Stone to Emerald. Rewards scale with your current depth: the deeper you go, the bigger the bonuses. Risk of cave-in (5–15%) resets you to the last checkpoint. A 4% chance to drop a **star** on each successful dig.

### /dungeon
Enter a multi-encounter dungeon run every **hour**. Face up to 5 encounters — monster combat, treasure chests, traps, and NPC merchants. Combat uses interactive buttons (attack, defend, run). HP persists across the full run. Same checkpoint system as mining. Also has a 4% star drop chance.

Both mini-games have checkpoints that protect your progress. See the [Mining Guide](/en/guide/mine) and [Dungeon Guide](/en/guide/dungeon) for full strategy.

## XP & Leveling

XP is earned **passively** — no commands needed:

- **Messages** — earn XP for every message you send (anti-spam: 60s cooldown, minimum 3 characters)
- **Voice chat** — earn XP per minute spent in voice channels
- **Reactions** — earn XP for reacting to messages (30s cooldown per channel)

Level up to receive coin and gem rewards automatically. Use `/rank` to see your rank card with level, XP progress bar, and server position. Use `/leaderboard` to see the top members in the server.

See the [XP Guide](/en/guide/xp) for configuration details and canvas rank cards.

## Daily Quests

Three quests are generated for you each day — one easy, one medium, one hard.

- `/quest view` — see today's quests and your current progress on each
- `/quest claim` — claim your bonus reward once all 3 quests are complete

Complete quests on consecutive days to build a quest streak with multiplied rewards.

See the [Quest Guide](/en/guide/quest) for the full quest pool and reward tables.

## Global Wallet & Stars

**Stars** ⭐ are a cross-server currency that works across all servers you're in — unlike coins and gems which are per-server.

- `/wallet daily` — claim your daily star reward (streak bonuses apply)
- `/wallet view` — check your star balance and lifetime milestones

Spend stars on manga commands and the global shop. Stars cannot be transferred between users or adjusted by admins.

See the [Star Guide](/en/guide/star) and [Global Shop Guide](/en/guide/global-shop).

## Confessions

Post anonymous messages to the server's confession channel:

- `/confession` — submit a text confession (image or audio attachments available on premium)
- The community can upvote, downvote, and reply — all anonymously

Server admins can enable approval mode so confessions go through a mod review before posting.

See the [Confessions Guide](/en/guide/confessions) for the full system.

## Voice Channels

Join the designated join-to-create channel to get your own **temporary voice channel** instantly. A control panel appears with full management options:

- Lock or hide the channel
- Rename it and set a user limit
- Permit or block specific members

When everyone leaves, the channel is automatically deleted.

See the [Voice Guide](/en/guide/voice) for setup and panel controls.

## Premium

Star and Galaxy tiers unlock reduced cooldowns, higher rewards, exclusive features (like audio confessions), and bonus limits across all systems.

Use `/premium compare` to see the full side-by-side comparison of all tier benefits.

See the [Premium Guide](/en/guide/premium).
