## Subcommands

| Subcommand | Description | Example |
|------------|-------------|---------|
| `/quest view` | View today's quests and progress | `/quest view` |
| `/quest claim` | Claim your all-quests-complete bonus | `/quest claim` |

## How It Works

Every day you receive **3 random quests** — 1 easy, 1 medium, 1 hard. Complete them by using bot commands as you normally would. When all 3 are done, claim your star bonus.

### Quest Difficulties

| Difficulty | Examples | Coin Reward |
|-----------|---------|-------------|
| Easy | Pray for someone, check rank, view wallet | 10 coin |
| Medium | Work, fish, mine, gift coins | 20 coin |
| Hard | Complete a dungeon, mine 2 times, win a gamble | 35 coin |

Coin rewards are paid automatically when each quest completes. Premium tiers earn more coins per quest.

### All-Complete Bonus

Complete all 3 quests and use `/quest claim` to receive **+1 star** (Free), **+2 star** (Star tier), or **+3 star** (Galaxy tier).

### Quest Streak

Complete all quests on consecutive days to build a streak. Milestones at 3, 7, 14, and 30 days give bonus stars.

> **Tip:** Quests reset at UTC midnight. Do your easy quest first for quick momentum!
