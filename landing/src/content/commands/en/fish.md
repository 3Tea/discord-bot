## Usage

```
/fish
```

## How It Works

Cast your line and catch a random fish! Each catch has a rarity tier:

| Rarity | Chance | Coin Reward | Example Fish |
|--------|--------|-------------|-------------|
| 🐟 Common | 55% | 10–30 | Sardine, Carp, Mackerel |
| 🐠 Uncommon | 28% | 40–80 | Salmon, Tuna, Bass |
| 🐡 Rare | 13% | 100–200 | Pufferfish, Swordfish, Lobster |
| 🦈 Legendary | 4% | 300–600 | Shark, Whale, Golden Koi |

Coins are awarded instantly — no need to sell your catch. The embed shows your fish name, rarity, and reward.

> **Tip:** With a 1-hour cooldown, you can fish up to 24 times a day. Legendary catches are rare but worth the wait!

### Server Config
Admins can adjust cooldown and reward multiplier via `/economy work-config-*` commands.
