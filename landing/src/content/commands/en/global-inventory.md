## Subcommands

| Subcommand | Description | Example |
|------------|-------------|---------|
| `/global-inventory view` | View your owned items | `/global-inventory view` |

## How to Use

Use `/global-inventory view` to see all items you've purchased from the global shop. Each entry shows:

- **Item ID** — the item identifier
- **Quantity** — how many you own
- **Last obtained** — when you most recently purchased it

Items are sorted by most recently obtained first. Use the `page` option to navigate (10 items per page).

> **Note:** Your global inventory is the same across all servers — items purchased in one server are available everywhere.
