## Subcommands

| Subcommand | Description | Example |
|------------|-------------|---------|
| `/premium status` | View your current tier, expiry, and active benefits | `/premium status` |
| `/premium compare` | Side-by-side comparison of Free vs Star vs Galaxy | `/premium compare` |

## How to Use

### /premium status

Shows your current premium tier (Star, Galaxy, or Free), expiration date, and a summary of your active benefits including manga free uses, max pages, star drop multiplier, and daily bonus stars.

- If you have premium: displays your tier with a colored embed (gold for Star, purple for Galaxy) and your benefits
- If you don't have premium: shows a prompt to use `/premium compare` to see what's available

### /premium compare

Displays a full comparison table of all three tiers — Free, Star, and Galaxy — covering:

- Manga free uses per day and max pages
- Cooldown reductions for work, fish, mine, and dungeon
- Star drop rate bonus
- Confession perks (free skip cooldown, free VIP)
- Daily bonus stars
- Rank card badge and theme

> **Tip:** Use `/premium compare` to decide which tier fits your playstyle before purchasing.
